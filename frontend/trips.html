How Swarm Planning Works

Our system doesn't generate raw text template itineraries. It coordinates API integrations to fetch actual stays and transit costs, and uses five distinct cognitive layers to compile details.

1

Submit Route Details

Provide source, destination, date, and budget limit. Set vibe matches like culture, dining, or beaches.

2

Swarm Deployment

The Coordinator deploys sub-agent workers to source hotels, compare flights, check weather trends, and calculate costs.

3

Get Boarding Pass Summary

Once audits finish, your final boarding pass workspace renders with day timeline accordions and circular budget stats gauges.
